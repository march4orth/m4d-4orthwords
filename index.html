      How to Play

      1. 1. Pick 9 letters. Choose Vowel or Consonant nine times (or press V / C) to fill the board.
      2. 2. Beat the clock. The instant your 9th letter lands, a 60-second round begins automatically.
      3. 3. Tap to spell. Tap letters on the board in order to build a word (3+ letters), then tap Enter. Tap a staged letter again to remove it. Find as many words as you can before time runs out.
      4. 4. Longer scores more. Points escalate with word length — a 9-letter word is worth far more than a 3-letter one.
      5. 5. Review the round. When time's up, see your score, why any misses didn't count, and the single longest word possible from that letter set.
